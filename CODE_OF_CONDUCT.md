# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be professional. Be direct. Help people build things. Don't be a problem.

---

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Communicate clearly and respectfully
- Give and receive feedback without making it personal
- Help others when you can — everyone was a beginner once
- Stay on topic — this is a developer tool, keep discussions technical and constructive
- Credit others' work and contributions

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or behavior based on race, gender, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other personal characteristic
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project, or bad-faith contributions
- Using the project or community to facilitate fraud, illegal activity, or harm to others
- Attempting to exploit vulnerabilities in other contributors' projects or systems

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, the AgenticMarket Discord or community channels if established, and any other official communication channel related to this project.

It also applies when someone is representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to **support@agenticmarket.dev**.

All reports are reviewed privately. We will:

1. Acknowledge the report within 48 hours
2. Investigate the situation
3. Take appropriate action — which may include a warning, temporary restriction, or permanent ban depending on severity

We will not tolerate retaliation against anyone who reports a violation in good faith.

Maintainers who do not enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face consequences as determined by project leadership.

---

## Malpractice

Using AgenticMarket tooling, infrastructure, or community to:

- Scrape, spam, or abuse third-party APIs in violation of their terms
- Build servers designed to exfiltrate user data
- Circumvent billing, authentication, or rate limiting on any platform
- Deceive users about what an MCP server does
- Publish malicious tools disguised as legitimate MCP servers

...will result in immediate and permanent removal from the community, revocation of AgenticMarket platform access, and where applicable, reporting to relevant authorities.
